Hakone's Quiet Side

You did the ropeway and the pirate ship. Here's the Hakone you missed by not staying the night.

The Second Trip

After the Tour Buses Leave

Hakone is worth the overnight. One to two nights in a ryokan with a private outdoor bath and kaiseki dinner. Prices start around ¥15,000 for a tatami room and the shared onsen, up to ¥50,000+ for a private rotenburo and dinner served in your room.

Stay near a station on the Hakone Tozan Line so you can explore the area between soaks. The ropeway and pirate ship loop gets packed by mid-morning, but the onsen are the reason to come back.
