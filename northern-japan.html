Northern Japan

Japan's best seafood, mountain onsen, and Hokkaido dairy. Tohoku and southern Hokkaido by rail.

The Second Trip

Your Base in the North

Two hubs, one region. Sendai for Tohoku (90 min from Tokyo by Shinkansen). Sapporo for Hokkaido (90-min flight). Budget 3-4 days per hub, or a full week if you're doing both. The north is where Japan's ingredients come from: Hokkaido dairy, Tohoku beef and rice, seafood pulled from cold water the same morning. Regional passes (JR East Tohoku Pass, JR Hokkaido Rail Pass) cover the distances. The gap between Sendai and Sapporo is real. Most people pick one sub-region or fly between them.

Where to Base in the North

Two hub cities to base yourself, plus onsen towns, harbor cities, and castle towns worth the side trip.

90 min from Tokyo

Sendai

Your Tohoku Hub

Tohoku's largest city and your base for the north. Gyutan (beef tongue) is the signature: charcoal-grilled, thicker cuts than Tokyo, served with barley rice and tail soup. Day trips to Yamadera (60 min, mountain temple carved into a cliff) and Matsushima (40 min, island-dotted bay with temple walks). Business hotels near the station run around ¥8,000/night.

Your base for this region
3.5 hrs from Sapporo

Hakodate

Port City, Morning Market, Midnight Views

The morning market opens before dawn: squid pulled live from the tank, uni bowls, seafood donburi for breakfast. The ropeway night view is one of Japan's best, and the observation deck empties by 9pm. The Meiji-era Western quarter has brick warehouses converted to shops and restaurants. One night minimum; two to eat properly.

2 nights recommended
75 min from Sapporo

Noboribetsu

Hokkaido's Hell Valley & Volcanic Hot Springs

Boardwalks through sulfur vents and boiling mud pools, with onsen hotels fifteen minutes away. The baths are fed directly by the volcanic springs beneath them: different minerals, different colors, different temperatures. The main street gets busy. The volcanic hiking trails behind it don't. Day trip from Sapporo (75 min) or stay one night.

1 night recommended
