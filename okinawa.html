Okinawa & the Ryukyu Islands

Beach and reef Japan. Different food, different culture, different pace from the mainland.

The Second Trip

Your Base in Okinawa

Fly from mainland Japan (2-2.5 hours from Tokyo or Osaka). Budget at least 5 days: 2 in Naha, 3 on the outer islands. A week is better. This is not mainland Japan. The food, architecture, and culture are Ryukyuan, and the travel logic is completely different. Outside Naha, you need a car on the main island and ferries or flights between the outer islands. Book both ahead in summer.

Stay Overnight

Where to Base in Okinawa

Naha as your hub, plus outer island beaches and jungle across the Ryukyu chain. Most destinations need a car or ferry.

2.5 hrs from Tokyo

Naha

The Ryukyuan Capital

Okinawa's only city with real public transit. The Yui Rail monorail runs from the airport through Kokusai-dori to Shuri Castle. The food is completely different from mainland Japan: goya champuru, soki soba, taco rice, and awamori. IC cards work on the monorail and buses since 2020. Hotels near Kokusai-dori run ¥5,000-10,000/night.

35 min ferry from Naha

Kerama Islands

World-Class Beaches, 30 Minutes Away

Zamami and Tokashiki: two small islands with white sand beaches, 35-50 minutes by ferry from Naha. Sea turtles in the shallows, visibility past 30 meters, and islands small enough to bike in an hour. Guesthouses include meals. Book ferries ahead in summer and Golden Week; they sell out.

1–2 nights recommended Browse Guesthouses
1 hr from Naha

Ishigaki & Yaeyama Islands

Island-Hopping Hub of the South

Base in Ishigaki's walkable town center and ferry-hop to Taketomi (10 min, traditional village you bike in an hour), Iriomote (40 min, jungle), and Kohama. The town has excellent Yaeyama soba and ishigaki beef. Direct flights from Tokyo (3 hrs) and Osaka. A week on Ishigaki alone is more than enough.

3–5 nights recommended Browse Stays
45 min from Naha

Miyako-jima

Japan's Best Beaches

The best beaches in Japan. White sand, turquoise water, and coral reefs you can wade to. Three bridges connect to smaller islands with their own beaches. A loop bus and hotel shuttles exist, but a car opens up the island significantly. Direct flights from Tokyo (3 hrs) and Osaka.

2–3 nights recommended Browse Resorts
2.5 hrs bus from Naha

Churaumi & Motobu

The Aquarium Coast

The whale shark tank at Churaumi Aquarium is worth the 2-hour bus ride from Naha alone. Express buses run multiple times daily. Staying overnight at the resort hotels nearby gives you Emerald Beach and Ocean Expo Park at a relaxed pace. Beyond the aquarium area, a car helps.

1–2 nights recommended Browse Resorts
40 min ferry from Ishigaki

Iriomote

UNESCO Jungle Island

Ninety percent jungle, UNESCO World Heritage since 2021. Mangrove kayaking, waterfall trekking, and beaches with nobody on them. Pre-book guided tours: they include hotel pickup, which matters because all taxi companies on the island have closed. The bus runs four times daily. Come for the adventure, not the convenience.
